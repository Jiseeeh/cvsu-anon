Rules and Guidelines

Respect and Tolerance: Treat all users with respect and kindness. Do not engage in bullying, harassment, or any form of hate speech. Discriminatory behavior is not tolerated.
Anonymity: Embrace the spirit of anonymity. Do not attempt to reveal anyone's identity or request personal information. This includes not sharing your own personal information.
Positive and Constructive Messages: Share messages that contribute to a positive and constructive atmosphere. Encourage open communication and healthy discussions.
No Inappropriate Content: Do not post or share content that is sexually explicit, offensive, violent, or in any way inappropriate. This includes text, images, or links.
No Spam: Avoid spamming the platform with repetitive or irrelevant messages. This disrupts the user experience and is not allowed.
Responsible Use: Use the point system and gamification features responsibly. Do not engage in any unethical or harmful practices to gain points.
Report Abusive Behavior: If you encounter abusive or inappropriate behavior, report it to the platform administrators immediately.
No Impersonation: Do not impersonate other users or any organization, including the university.
Privacy: Be cautious about what you share, even in an anonymous environment. Avoid sharing sensitive or harmful information.
No Unauthorized Use: Do not use the platform for any illegal activities, including hacking, spreading malware, or any other malicious actions.
Respect the Platform: Follow any additional rules or guidelines provided by the platform administrators and adhere to their decisions and sanctions.
